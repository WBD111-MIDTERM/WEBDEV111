Luna Vista Private Resort in Pansol, Calamba, Laguna, is a modern, luxurious, and popular venue for team buildings, birthdays, and family outings.
Featuring a sleek design with wood and stone, it offers adult and kiddie pools with panoramic views, air-conditioned rooms, a videoke setup, billiards, and barbecue area.

Key Features and Amenities of Luna Vista (Pansol):
Accommodations: Typically features 4 to 5 air-conditioned rooms with private or shared baths, often accommodating large groups (up to 30-50+ guests).
Pool Area: Modern swimming pool, often including a hot spring, jacuzzi (in some units), and a kiddie section. Recreation: Unlimited karaoke (videoke), billiards, basketball hoops, dart boards, and sometimes table tennis. Facilities: Kitchen area, refrigerator, gas stove (usually with a fee), water dispenser, and grilling area. Views: Known for overlooking views of the surrounding area in Laguna. Facebook Facebook +5 Booking and Information: Location: Sampaguita St., Purok 5, Brgy. Pansol, Calamba City, Laguna. Reservation: Requires a 50% down payment to secure dates. Variants: There are multiple listings for "Luna Vista" in Pansol, including Luna Vista 1 and Luna Vista 2, often managed via Facebook groups. Note: There is also a "Luna Vista Hotel" in Belek (Turkey) scheduled to open in 2025 and "Luna Vista Apartments" in Mountain View, California, but the search results primarily describe the private resort in Laguna, Philippines.
